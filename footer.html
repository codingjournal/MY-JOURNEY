When, Why and How to use <footer> tag

Footer Tag is a semantic tag like a Header Tag. It gives a structure to the layout of your web pages. It is the bottom or you can say concluding section of your web pages. Search Engines use the footer to identify copyright data, links to terms and conditions, authorship etc. It helps search engines to index your webpage properly. Footer tag can be used in two different way.
  • Page Level Footer. It is at the bottom of your web page
  • Article or Section Level Footer. It represent the conclusion of your Article or content.
